Dina's story
Dina’s melanoma diagnosis was daunting, but she told her doctor that she was determined to see her teenage daughter graduate from high school. Eventually, she had to set new goals, seeing her daughter move on from high school to college to nursing school and beyond. Learn how her cancer journey has led her to find profound joy in every moment, every day.
