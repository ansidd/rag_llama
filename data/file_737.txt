Meet Chris
Chris always admired the resilience of children and had to channel that resilience following his cancer diagnosis. Thanks to a treatment option from Bristol Myers Squibb, Chris is now able to live his life's passion helping patients, like him, at local hospitals.

I am not a cancer patient. I am a person who happens to have cancer.
