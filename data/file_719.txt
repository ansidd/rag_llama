Meet Anna
Anna and Monica had been friends since childhood, sharing 25 years of milestones together. Even when Monica was diagnosed with cancer, she never lost hope. Monica wanted to continue to celebrate life’s happy moments, including the announcement of Anna’s pregnancy. Now, those memories of Monica drive Anna to do her best work at Bristol Myers Squibb.

She was always positive and thinking one day she would be cured.
