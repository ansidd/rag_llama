Meet Beth
Within weeks of starting a girl scout troop in her community, Beth was diagnosed with cancer.  She was determined not to let her cancer diagnosis define her. Thanks to a Bristol Myers Squibb treatment option, Beth is now proudly leading a troop of 45 girls scouts and serving her community.

I felt in my heart that I was going to be able to tackle this.
