At Bristol Myers Squibb, we are doing everything we can to ensure that patients with serious or life-threatening diseases receive the benefit of our innovative medicines as quickly as possible.
Submitting a request form
We welcome requests for Pre-Approval Access (PAA) from the physicians treating these patients. Request forms must be submitted by the healthcare professional. Patient eligibility will be determined by Bristol Myers Squibb in accordance with established policies and procedures. Bristol Myers Squibb’s acceptance and processing of this application does not guarantee that access to the investigational product will be provided.
