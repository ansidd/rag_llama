Meet Sandy
Sandy was heartbroken when her then 19-month old son was diagnosed with a life-threatening condition. As an employee at Bristol Myers Squibb for over 25 years, Sandy remains focused and motivated as she works for patients, Like her son, every day.  Now thriving in the life she always wanted for him, Sandy is thankful for the treatment her son received.
“
There’s nothing harder to see than your baby so sick.
