Emma Charles
Bristol Myers Squibb Italy was ranked 7th in the Best Workplaces™ Italy 2022, in the category of companies with more than 500 employees. The ranking is drawn up every year by the consulting and research institute Great Place to Work® Italy to reward and celebrate companies that put the wellbeing of their employees first.
“
“I truly believe that people are the engine of our success, and we  continue to encourage a stimulating and inclusive working environment.”
Emma Charles, General Manager
”
I am very proud of this achievement and would like to thank the Italian organization for having participated in the survey and for the feedback that allowed us to reach the important recognition. I truly believe that people are the engine of our success, and we continue to encourage a stimulating and inclusive working environment.
