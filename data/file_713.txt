Meet Fady
Finding out that someone close to you has cancer is difficult to reconcile. When three members of your extended family all receive cancer diagnoses in a short period of time, your outlook on life can shift dramatically. For Fady, this news prompted him to refocus on what’s truly important—living life with purpose. He left his long-time job to join Bristol Myers Squibb as a financial professional, where he now feels he’s part of something special.
“
I kind of impact [people's lives], even if I’m in a support function.
”
